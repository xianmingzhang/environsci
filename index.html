Xianming Zhang

PhD in Environmental Chemistry

Welcome to my website!

I am currently a research associate in environmental science and engineering at the John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, Harvard University.

I gained my PhD and MSc degrees from the University of Toronto and BSc from Peking University.

My overarching research interest revolves around environmental chemistry, environmental modeling and imapct assessment of anthropogenic toxic substances.

My research aims to gain mechanistic understanding on chemical emissions, environmental fate and bioaccumulation processes and the implications on human exposure and health outcomes.

I develop environmental sampling tools, methods of instrumental analysis, chemical fate and exposure models and use integrated methods to achieve my research objectives.
